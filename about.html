NOT YOUR SIN is a name that came to me literally in a dream and instantly I knew that it was the perfect name!

Well, let me introduce myself.
Maria is my name and I am from a small country, born in an even smaller village. I grew up in a non-artistic family but my passion for art was there since day one. Somehow though, along the way I got a BSc. in Civil and Environmental Engineering, which I never used, because of my love for art!! I also quitted my Master in beautiful Denmark, and the opportunity to have a rich Engineering career and taught myself traditional art and digital design.

I cannot imagine my life without creating. I'd love to experiment with lots of materials not only paint and pixels. Currently, I am trying out Jesmonite and the journey is unexpected and exciting!
